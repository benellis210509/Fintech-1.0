2. Information collected

We may collect your name, email address, password hash, login and account records, subscription status, uploaded portfolio CSV data, holdings, values, watchlist notes, settings, reports, technical logs and information you provide when contacting us. Stripe handles payment-card details; the service does not store full card numbers.

3. Why information is used

Information is used to create and secure your account, provide portfolio dashboards and reports, maintain separate user records, process subscriptions, respond to support and privacy requests, prevent misuse, diagnose errors and improve the service.

4. Service providers and overseas processing

The service may send limited information to service providers used for hosting, payments, AI text generation and market data. These may include Render, Stripe, OpenAI and Yahoo Finance or related data providers. Some processing may occur outside New Zealand. Do not upload information you are not authorised to disclose.

5. AI and market data

When AI commentary is enabled, portfolio totals, structural flags and mechanical research indicators may be sent to the configured AI provider. Market tickers are sent to third-party market-data services. Market data can be delayed, incomplete or inaccurate.

6. Storage and retention

We retain information only while reasonably required for the purposes above, legal obligations, security and dispute resolution. Raw CSV uploads are deleted after synchronous processing. Reports and account data are retained until deletion or another documented retention rule applies. The current SQLite/local-file deployment is suitable for controlled testing only and must be replaced or configured with reliable persistent storage before production use.

7. Security

Reasonable safeguards are used, including hashed passwords, signed sessions, access separation and transport security in production. No internet service is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential.

8. Access, correction and deletion

You may request access to or correction of your personal information, or request account deletion, by contacting the privacy officer. Identity verification may be required before a request is completed.

9. Privacy breaches

We will assess suspected privacy breaches and notify affected people and the New Zealand Privacy Commissioner when required by law.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes should be communicated through the service or by email, and the version date will be updated.
