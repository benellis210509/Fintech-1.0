Financial Information Disclaimer

No financial advice

Fintech is a general research and educational tool. It does not provide regulated or personalised financial advice, investment planning, product suitability assessments, brokerage services or trading instructions.

No buy, sell or hold recommendation

Scores, flags, indicators, grades and AI-generated summaries are mechanical descriptions of limited data. They are not recommendations or opinions that you should acquire, dispose of, retain or change any particular financial product.

Incomplete personal context

The service does not know your complete financial position, objectives, debts, dependants, tax position, investment horizon, capacity for loss or liquidity requirements. It cannot determine what is appropriate for you.

Data and model risk

Uploaded information and third-party market data may be delayed, incomplete or wrong. AI-generated text can contain errors. Historical information and model scores do not predict future performance.

Snapshot changes are not returns

Changes between uploaded portfolio totals are not calculated investment returns unless cash flows, trades, fees, tax, currency movements and timing are correctly adjusted. The current service does not perform that full calculation.

Obtain qualified help

Before making a financial decision, verify the information and consider consulting a licensed New Zealand financial advice provider, accountant, tax adviser or lawyer as appropriate.
